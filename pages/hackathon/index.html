The lightning web virtual hackathon is a 7 day event for designers and frontend developers to collaborate and build new lightning web applications using WebLN, LNURL, and Lightning Address. The week will be filled with guest speakers, workshops, design sprints, and more. Enter your own team of up to 5 members, or be paired up with others who compliment your skill set. At the end of the 7 days, pitch your project with a short 10 video to be examined by our panel of judges. Prizes will be given to 1st, 2nd, and 3rd places! If you're ready to build the next layer of the web, come and join us at our Shock the Web hackaton on Nov 10-17th, 2021.
